Sign up as a student to start your lessons.

3.Edit Profile
Enter your personal information on My Page > Profile page.
Details of your profile is only open to tutors.
4.Purchase Tickets
All lessons at U&I Language can be scheduled with tickets.
There are lesson tickets and nomination tickets.
Nomination tickets will be required when you designate a tutor for a lesson (service under development).

Lesson tickets are required for scheduling lessons:
One ticket for a 20-minute S class lesson
Two tickets for a 45-minute M class lesson
5. Lesson Request
The basic system of U&I Language requires the tutor to make an offer according to the student's needs. We want to help students avoid spending a lot of time searching for a tutor and starting lessons according to the tutor's time.

Feel free to enter the time(s) you want your lesson and the content of the lesson on the Lesson Request page.
Then, tutors who fit your needs will make suggestions (offers) for your requested lesson(s).
6. Select Your Tutor
Please check the offers from the tutors on the Lesson Request page.
Check the profiles of tutors and choose the tutor that suits you best, and you're done!
7.Start Lesson
You can check your reserved lessons on the Study Lessons page.
You can check the Google Meet link registered by the tutor on the Lesson Details page.
Enjoy native English-speaking tutors and high-quality English courses tailored to your needs!

Click the page refresh button to update the link before click Join Lesson.
If you can't connect with the button, press the copy icon to copy the link and enter it directly.
8-1. After a lesson, if you like the tutor, please use the nomination function (service under development).
A perfect match with a tutor! If you want to continue taking classes with this tutor, please use the nomination function.
A nomination ticket will be required for nomination lessons.

Nomination ticket(s) required for nomination:
One ticket for a 20-minute S class lesson,
Two tickets for a 45-minute M class lesson

If you request a nomination lesson by entering the desired time and content to your designated tutor, the lesson will be confirmed once the tutor confirms and approves. If a nomination lesson is requested without prior agreement, the nomination lesson may be not be approved due to the circumstances of the tutor.
8-2. After a lesson, if you want to try other lessons with another tutor?
Request lesson again by entering your needs in details.
A tutor who is perfect match that you haven't met yet will surely find you soon.
